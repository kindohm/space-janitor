Space Janitor - Info

Space Janitor is a throwback, near-clone of the good old, popular Asteroids arcade game.

This game is in heavy development. What you see today may be very different tomorrow.

Scoring

  • 100 - big asteroids
  • 250 - medium asteroids
  • 500 - small asteroids
  • 1000 - UFO
  • Level Bonus = Level Number * 500 + Shot % * 1000;

Technical

  • Source code: github.com/kindohm/space-janitor

  • Built with JavaScript and the following libraries:

    • Coquette
    • Howler
  • All sounds crafted from scratch using FL Studio
  • Some blog posts on building this game are at kindohm.com/blog

Space Janitor appears to perform the best in Chrome and Internet Explorer. Firefox appears to lag behind sometimes. This isn't deliberate and I aim to get the game to work equally well in these three browsers.
